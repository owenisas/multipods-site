MultiPods

Share your Mac's audio with multiple AirPods and Bluetooth headphones — like iPhone's Share Audio, but for macOS.

Features

  • Share audio to 2–4 Bluetooth devices simultaneously
  • Works with AirPods, AirPods Pro, AirPods Max, Beats, Sony, Bose
  • Per-device volume control
  • Battery level display for AirPods
  • Lives in the menu bar — lightweight and always ready
  • Launch at Login so it's always available
