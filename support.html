FAQ

How do I connect a friend's AirPods? Have your friend put their AirPods in the case, keep the lid open, and hold the button on the back until the light flashes white. Then go to your Mac's Bluetooth settings and connect them.
How many devices can I share audio with? macOS reliably supports 3–4 simultaneous Bluetooth audio devices. Performance may vary with more.
Why is there a slight delay between devices? Bluetooth audio has 80–220ms latency. This is fine for music and video, but may be noticeable in real-time applications.
My audio stopped when I removed an AirPod AirPods have automatic ear detection — removing one triggers a disconnect. MultiPods detects this and will either rebuild the audio sharing with remaining devices or stop sharing if fewer than 2 devices remain.
The app icon disappeared from my menu bar Make sure MultiPods is running. Enable "Launch at Login" in the app's footer to ensure it starts automatically.
Audio didn't restore after stopping sharing MultiPods saves your original output device and restores it when you stop sharing. If your original device disconnected in the meantime, it falls back to your Mac's built-in speakers.
