Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Overview

MultiPods is designed with privacy in mind. The app operates entirely on your Mac and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

MultiPods does not collect any data. Specifically:

• No personal information is collected
• No usage analytics or telemetry
• No crash reports sent externally
• No network requests of any kind
• No accounts or sign-in required

Local Storage

The app stores your preferences locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults. This includes your selected audio devices and volume settings. This data never leaves your device and is automatically removed if you delete the app.

Bluetooth Access

MultiPods requests Bluetooth access solely to display battery levels for your connected audio devices (e.g., AirPods left/right/case percentages). No Bluetooth data is transmitted externally.

Audio Access

MultiPods uses macOS Core Audio APIs to create multi-output audio devices for sharing audio between multiple Bluetooth headphones. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

MultiPods does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics frameworks.

Children's Privacy

MultiPods does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the support page.
